Prague, Czech Republic

Prague Vintage Car Tour

As an avid car enthusiast, I have always associated Prague with my love for cars. I remember growing up playing racing games (Forza) and running laps throughout the picturesque streets of Prague. Even in movies, Prague is associated with the car scene as seen in Xander Cage. However, beyond the car scene, Prague just has a very distinct atmosphere eminating from it. A Czech company, Prague Old Car, decided to incorporate Prague's car atmosphere with the city's tourist attractions. Their car tour allows tourists to experience Prague in a new way. According to the company, Prague and beautiful cars always go together, so why not experience them together. There are also a variety of cars to choose from, including the Model A, Model T, and Spider. The goal of this company is sllowing tourists to experience the old magical city of Prague in comfort without rushing or stress.
